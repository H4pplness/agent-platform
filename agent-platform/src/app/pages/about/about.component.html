Mission & Vision

Why we exist

Our Mission

To empower every team — from startups to Fortune 500 — with intelligent automation that works as hard as they do. We believe AI agents should be a tool for everyone, not just those with deep technical expertise.

"Make AI automation as natural as writing an email."

Our Vision

A world where human creativity is amplified by AI — where people spend their time on what matters most, and intelligent agents handle the rest. We're building toward a future where every business runs on AI-native workflows.

"The best AI is one you forget is there."

The Story

How we got here

In 2021, Alex Morgan and Jamie Liu were both running large AI teams at their respective companies — Alex at Google, Jamie at Amazon. They kept running into the same problem: building AI-powered automation was expensive, slow, and required armies of ML engineers.

They believed it didn't have to be that way. In early 2022, they left their jobs, moved into a shared apartment in San Francisco, and started building what would become AgentAI.

The first version was rough — a command-line tool with five commands and zero UI. But the early users loved the idea. Within six months, the team had grown to 10 people, closed a seed round, and shipped a product that real teams were using every day.

Today, AgentAI powers automation for over 500 companies across 30+ countries. We've never lost sight of why we started: to give every team the superpower of intelligent automation.

"We didn't quit our jobs to build another SaaS tool. We quit to solve a problem that was quietly costing every company millions."

— Alex Morgan, CEO & Co-founder
